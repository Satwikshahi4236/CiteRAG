# Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

For **enterprise customers**, our standard SLA guarantees:

- **99.9% uptime** measured monthly
- **P1 incident response within 1 hour**
- **P2 incident response within 4 business hours**

For **growth customers**, our default SLA is:

- **99.5% uptime** measured monthly
- **P1 incident response within 4 hours**
- **P2 incident response within 1 business day**

All SLAs are documented in the internal SLA policy document and may be customized on a per-contract basis.
